Return Information

Return window: Any unopened or unused grocery items can be returned within 14 days after their purchase date.

Refunds Processing: Once the return is received and checked, the refund will be processed with store credit or the original payment method within 7 business days.

Return Shipping: If the return happens because of a mistake on our side (such as wrong items sent, or damaged product), we will take care of the return label with a prepaid option. Otherwise, you will be responsible for the return process.

Non-returnable Items

  • Perishable products: Meat, seafood, dairy products, and fresh products unless damaged or defective.
  • Opened/used items: A product that has been used partially is not returnable.

This policy is designed for one year from the indicated date on the Sal FreshMart Grocery.
